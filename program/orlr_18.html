--- layout: paper id: 18 slides_live_id: 1 rocket_id: orlr-paper-18 meeting_url: authors: "Haozhi Qi, Xiaolong Wang, Deepak Pathak, Yi Ma, and Jitendra Malik" camera_ready: false cmt_id: 18 kind: poster session_id: 1 session_title: "Session TBD" title: "Learning Long-term Visual Dynamics with Region Proposal Interaction Networks" abstract: "Learning long-term dynamics models is the key to understanding physical common sense. Most existing approaches on learning dynamics from visual input sidestep long-term predictions by resorting to rapid re-planning with short-term models. This not only requires such models to be super accurate but also limits them only to tasks where an agent can continuously obtain feedback and take action \textit{at each step} until completion. In this paper, we aim to leverage the ideas from success stories in visual recognition tasks to build object representations that can capture inter-object and object-environment interactions over a long range. To this end, we propose Region Proposal Interaction Networks (RPIN), which reason about each object's trajectory in a latent region-proposal feature space. Our approach outperforms prior methods by a significant margin both in terms of prediction quality and their ability to plan for downstream tasks, and also generalize well to novel environments. Results are available at https://sites.google.com/view/orlr-workshop-rpin." track: research live: false video_file_url: none youtube_url: none ---
